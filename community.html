Community Leadership

  • 2026

    Labs Coordinator

    Intercollegiate Psychedelics Network (IPN)

    My time serving as President of Terrace and PsiClub centered my heart in community, and IPN is a wonderful contiunation of this path. As IPN Labs, we host a monthly seminar speaker series spanning altered states, plants, consciousness research, and the integration of quantum science. We connect students and researchers across institutions working at the frontiers of psychedelic and consciousness science, forging a new path forward for academia, industry, and indepdent organizations alike.

  • 2025

    President

    Princeton Science of Psychedelics Club (PsiClub), Princeton University

    During my senior year, with the help of friends we revived ψ Club from dormancy into a town wide community, ranging from students to faculty to members of the town of Princeton. We hosted weekly seminar sequence called ψ Series from pioneers of the psychedelic field. Speakers include Dennis McKenna, Sam Wang, Glenn Shepard, and Alexander Randall V. Created conversation around medical and ritual history and future of psychedelics. Evolved local narrative surrounding psychedelics, science, history, culture, and religion.

  • 2024

    President

    Terrace F. Club, Princeton University

    My junior year, I was asked to run for President of Terrace, Princeton's longtime muscial epicenter and home that has always made room for the unconventional. I was elected in November 2023 and served through November 2024. This position changed my life in many ways, it broke me down and built me up, showed me the magic of life, and most significantly soothed my soul with the sound of music. Our officer corps hosted weekly live rock concerts on Thursday nights and raves on Saturdays, plus other events ranging from art markets to haunted houses. Parties got up to 2000+ people!
