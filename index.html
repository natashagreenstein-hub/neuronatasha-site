About

What happens when the great mysteries of life converge on each other? When the subtle way of the Dao guides us towards the ever present yet ever fleeting moments of infinite quantum fields? When the curves and whorls of psychedelic experiences mirror the curves and whorls of our lungs, our veins, the stars, the trees? What happens when the great mysteries of life converge? We begin to see patterns in the way things behave. Trends in the way things function And in our endless pursuit to quantify these phenomena, we find ourselves at the geometric representation of interdisciplinary junction

From theoretical validation of quantum biology experimental results to computational fractal analysis mergings scales of complex systems, the core of my projects is to answer these questions empirically. For example, how does quantum information express itself in neurobiological systems? Are magnetic fields of biological orders of magnitude sufficient for altering information on quantum computers? Trained in the Physics Department of Princeton University for my Bachelor's and I'm currently completing my Master's in Brain and Cognition at Universitat Pompeu Fabra in Barcelona. Curious? Let's connect with Nature and explore the depths of the Universe together.
