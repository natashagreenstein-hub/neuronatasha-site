Mapping the landscapes of interdisciplinary convergence

What happens when the great mysteries of life converge on each other? When the subtle way of the Dao guides us towards the ever present yet ever fleeting moments of infinite quantum fields? When the curves and whorls of psychedelic experiences mirror the curves and whorls of our lungs, our veins, the stars, the trees? What happens when the great mysteries of life converge? We begin to see patterns in the way things behave. Trends in the way things function. And in our endless pursuit to quantify these phenomena, we find ourselves at the geometric representation of interdisciplinary junction.
